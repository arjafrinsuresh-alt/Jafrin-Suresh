Airport Terminal Expansion Concept

A terminal designed for the sky.

A terminal designed for the sky.

This thesis project explores the conceptual expansion of an international airport terminal through modular design and parametric flow modeling. As a hypothetical proposal developed for Sathyabama University, it prioritizes intuitive passenger circulation and flexible infrastructure to accommodate future aviation demands.

The design research focuses on modular expansion workflows and passenger experience through technical resolution of building envelopes and circulation logic, optimizing efficiency in high-traffic aviation hubs.
